Medical & Physical Highlights

  • Visual Standards: Uncorrected visual acuity must match designated service margins (e.g., 6/6 correctable to 6/6 for active piloting paths).
  • Minimum Height: 157.5 cm (men) / 152 cm (women), with age-weight ratio scaling.
  • No history of chronic cardiorespiratory defects, severe musculoskeletal anomalies, or severe skin conditions.

SSB Interview Protocol

  • Stage 1: Officer Intelligence Rating (OIR) and Picture Perception & Discussion Test (PP&DT).
  • Stage 2: Psychological profiling, GTO outdoor tasks, and Personal Interviews.
  • Day 5: Closing board conference matching evaluation metrics.
